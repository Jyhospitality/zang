Zangpo-Ling

Hotel Zangpo-Ling is the kind of place where visitors look for in the hills. The place, which is partially surrounded by walls, provides a spectacular view of the entire city. Zangpo-Ling is located in Gangtok, which has a number of tourist attractions that provide a diverse range of experiences. This Gangtok hotel is only a 3.5 km away from the famous MG Marg.

Zangpo-Ling has Executive and Family rooms available for guests to choose from. The rooms in Gangtok have spacious interiors and breathtaking views that will instantly lift your spirits and transcend you to the blissful hilltops. The rooms are designed for comfort, with amenities such as television, telephone, hot and cold water, attached bathroom, and various other bathroom toiletries. The hotel has an additional set of outstanding facilities that promise a relaxed stay in a warm and comfortable environment.
